Astro-Nautical

GGJ 2021:

“Astro-Nautical is a zen fishing game, in space! As a helpful fishing astronaut, take requests to find lost items in the deep cosmic sea, yet it’s not as simple as casting and hooking, as space is a vast ecosystem full of creatures who like a good snack! By using other fish and items, give the aquatic animals things they like to hook them, and give them things they dislike to have them spit new things out. Use your handy Fishtionary to figure out a chain of likes and dislikes so that a fish spits out what you are looking for!”

Role:

During the concepting phase, served as a major contributor to conceptual design. As design progressed, worked to create the background system for progression as System Designer, linking the connections between fish and items to create unique paths a player can take. Additionally, worked as the writer for the Fishtionary, an encyclopedia for the fish within the game, giving all the fish unique names and descriptions.

Writing Samples:

Greenhouse Turtle:
"Accompanied by a little plant they grow on their shells, they spend most of their time in space looking for resources to tend to it. At birth, the parents will take a seed from their plant to pass onto the newborn.”

Adorned Space Lion:
"The tube you see around their neck is—in fact—not a part of their body. These space lions have learned and shared amongst themselves the joy of wearing the tube. Who wouldn’t like a fashionable scarf?""
