Ocean Story

Author’s note:
Written as an experimental piece about mental illness, I wanted to explore the topics of anxiety and suicide in a subtle and experiential way, one that doesn’t outline the horrors or turmoil that comes with such experiences and thoughts that I believe many other stories have done before. Rather, I wanted to write with a sense of beauty and mental growth that often opposes the typical thoughts, and to create a sense of emotional ambiguity until the very end where the actual decision is revealed. I in no way advocate for suicide and simply accepting such mental struggles as futile—if anything, I wholly believe in the opposite. But with that in mind, please enjoy the story.
